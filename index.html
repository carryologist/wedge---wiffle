Lawn Golf Drinking Game (21+)

🎯 Setup

  • 9 "holes" marked with flags, cones, or sticks
  • One wedge & unique-colored wiffle ball per player
  • Course = backyard, field, or lawn with obstacles

🏌️ How To Play

  1. Tee Off: Everyone shoots toward the target in turn
  2. Scoring a Hole: Land within 1 stride (≈3 ft) of the flag, or hit the flagpole directly
  3. Taking Turns: Each stroke = 1 point. Lowest total after 9 holes wins

⚔️ Foiling (Croquet-Style)

If your ball strikes another, you Foil:

  • Send: Get a free swing to pitch the opponent's ball in any desired direction
  • Burn: Return the opponent's ball to the tee for that hole

Foiled Player Choice: Accept or take a 1-drink penalty (ball stays in original spot)

🍻 Drinking Rules

  • Foiled: Take a sip if you reject a foil
  • Hazards:
    • Overshoot (2+ strides past target) → +1 sip
    • Out of bounds (driveway, roof, etc.) → +1 sip & reset
    • Triple Bogey (3+ over par) → finish your drink before next hole

🌊 Waterfall Ceremony (After Each Hole)

  1. Players line up in order of strokes (lowest first)
  2. The lowest scorer begins drinking; no one else may stop until they do
  3. Once they stop, the next lowest may stop, and so on
  4. Highest scorer(s) end up drinking longest!

🏆 Victory

  • Stroke Play: Lowest total strokes after 9 holes wins
  • Loser(s) buy/mix a communal drink or do a camp dare
